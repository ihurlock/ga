Lazy Sundays - Chill Locations

Explore the backstreets and little alleyways around Peckham at the weeked and you'll be in for multiple surprise. Some of these surprises are pleasant and others not so pleasant. Below are some of our favourite places to relax.

Petitou is the perfect place to relax on a Sunday with a paper and freshly brewed coffee. Classics like Eggs benedict are a must try.

Anderson & Co is worth the trip, whether you're looking for a freshly baked loaf or a tranquil garden brunch

Duck Egg Cafe is good for dropping by after exploring North Cross Road Market on Saturday's

The General Store hosts delicacies from across Europe with fresh cheese, breads, tinned goods and anything else you would expect from a store transported back to the 1940s
