GO FORTH AND EAT

With all of the restaurant/eatery reviews below we've strived to provide something for everyone. Whether you're looking fora quick bite, a meal with drinks or a more formal affair; it's all there. Click on the images to visit the restaurant website or click "Full review" if you want the low-down from Peckham Sunsets.

The Flying Pig hosts a wide array of American microbrews as well as some delicious BBQ

The Peckham Refreshment Rooms has been buzzing since it opened. Head to Bar Story for happy hour beforehand for a delicious cocktail.

Ganapati is quickly becoming one of the most popular restaurants in South London.

McNeil's boasts some of the best chicken deals in South London. Plenty of Boss and plenty of spicy deals.

DRINKING FROM THE BOTTLE (glasses available)

Peckham, Camberwell and East Duwlich are so close together and contain some great places to taste cocktails, locally brewed beer and have a great time. We've attempted to encapsulate the range that the area offers in the below.

Bar Story is our favourite local bar, delicious cocktails as well as local brews from Kernel. Sit out the back by a wood fire during the winter or bask in the sun during the summertime.

The Peckham Pelican is a great location for chilling pre-party time. Bottles and cans at reasonable prices as well as Film and Art Events

The Victoria Inn is great for an evening drink, sitting on the terrace for great weekend breakfasts and Sunday Lunch

The Gowlett feels like a traditional pub but boasts delciious wood fired pizzas. The pub has been smoking their own cheeses for the pizza and curing meats for years.
